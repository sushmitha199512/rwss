SENIOR GEOLOGIST
 
To co-ordinate with the Superintending Engineer/ Executive Engineers, RWS/Projects in the District regarding the scientific source findings and related problems.
To monitor all drinking water source finalisation under RWS and Drought programmes.
To Monitor Geologists/Geophysicists reports and review the working of the Geologists in his jurisdiction.
To co-ordinate and monitor Pump tests for PWS schemes to evaluate Aquifer parameters.
To give guidance to the Geologists/ Geophysicists whenever required and assist in source finalisation for the chronic problematic habitations.
To monitor Geophysical Equipments and Geophysical investigations.
To collect and analyze the scientificdata related to Ground water and Surface water and prepare reports.
To maintain data base pertaining to the Geological investigations for the Water Supply Schemes.
 
JUNIOR GEOLOGITS
 
The source selection of sites for drinking water source development for all types of Water Supply Schemes and monitoring of these sources is the primary responsibility of the Junior Geologist. The scientific location of ground water sources for developing village water supply involved a multi-disciplinary approach and the following activities are envisaged.
 
a) To collect existing data/map pertaining to the area to be investigated.
b) To carry our Remote Sensing and Aerial Photograph data interpretation to derive the following information to supplement Ground Water investigations.
   
 
i. Geology and Structural Analysis
ii. Hydrogeomorphological studies
iii. Fracture and Dyke frequency Analysis
iv. Landuse/ Land cover
v. Soil types
   
c) To carry out hydrogeological investigations such as:
   
 
i. Well inventory for assessing the degree and extent of weathered and fractured zones and depth to basement and water level fluctuations.
ii. Water quality analysis
   
d) To carry out Geophysical surveys to estimate the physical properties of sub surface layers to pin point source for development.
e) To carry out Geo-hydrological investigations for locating structures to augment the Drinking water sources.
f) To carry out Pumping Test to arrive at Safe yield, depth of installation, capacity of pumps and for sustainable drinking water sources.
g) To maintain the date base on Ground water related parameters.
